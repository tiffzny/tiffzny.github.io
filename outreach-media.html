Outreach efforts & media appearances

The list below is not a full list, as I track down media appearances and such from over the years. Consider it a work-in-progress.

I strive to share “space stuff” as broadly as I can. There’s a sense of wonder, inspiration, and curiosity that can come from engaging with the vastness of the sky and the types of things we get to see within it: aurora, eclipses, meteor showers, beautiful changing phases of the moon, etc.

I’m grateful for the many opportunities I’ve had to engage with the public through BGO Tours and opportunities to engage with students through teaching, and there’s also something particularly unique and rewarding about participating in media: radio, TV, print, online that gets to engage and even larger community - those that may not seek out astro-information on their own.

Much of my outreach work is inspired by two mentors of mine: Dr. Rob Thacker and the late Dr. Dave Lane, who both have encouraged me endlessly in a variety of ways.
